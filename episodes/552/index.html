Level 1 Lesson 6 / Also, To go, Numbers / 也, 去, 数字



In this lesson you will love learning Chinese more because you will learn how to say “to go” somewhere.  You will also learn to tell others that you “also” love doing something or that you are “also” a fan of something.  We also added numbers so you should be able to count from 1 to 99.

Thank you for studying Chinese with us!

Your support is much appreciated in making this Chinese lesson Free!

Example Sentence and Dialogue to Practice

我也是中国人 wǒ yě shì zhōng guó rén I’m also Chinese.
我也是 wǒ yě shì So do I.
我也不喜欢咖啡 wǒ yě bù xǐ huan kā fēi I also don’t like coffee
我也不是日本人 wǒ yě bú shì rì běn rén Neither am I Japanese
我去日本 wǒ qù rì běn I’m going to Japan
我也去美国 wǒ yě qù měi guó I’m also going to USA
去吃饭 qù chī fàn going to eat

Dialogue 1

你是中国人吗? nǐ shì zhōng guó rén ma Are you Chinese?
我是中国人 wǒ shì zhōng guó rén I’m Chinese
我也是中国人 wǒ yě shì zhōng guó rén I’m also Chinese

Dialogue 2

你喜欢咖啡吗？ nǐ xǐ huan kā fēi ma Do you like coffee?
我不喜欢咖啡 wǒ bù xǐ huan kā fēi I don’t like coffee.
我也不喜欢咖啡 wǒ yě bù xǐ huan kā fēi I also don’t like coffee

Dialogue 3

我喜欢游泳，你呢？ wǒ xǐ huan yóu yǒng, nǐ ne I like swimming
我也喜欢游泳。你去游泳吗？ wǒ yě xǐ huan yóu yǒng. nǐ qù yóu yǒng ma I also like swimming. Are you going to go swimming?
不去，我去工作。你去游泳吗？ bú qù, wǒ qù gōng zuò. nǐ qù yóu yǒng ma (I’m) not going, I’m going to work. Are you going to go swimming?
我也不去游泳，我去吃饭。 wǒ yě bú qù yóu yǒng, wǒ qù chī fàn I’m also not going swimming, I’m going to go eat.
