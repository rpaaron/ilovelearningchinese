Level 1 Lesson 7 / 0, What time…., Weekends / 零, 现在几点了？, 你周末做什么?



You will love learning Chinese more because in this lesson you will be able to ask and tell time.  现在几点了？ What time is it now? You will also be able to tell us about your weekend.  你周末做什么?  What do you do on weekends?  Well what are you waiting for? Let’s listen to the lesson.

Key Vocabulary for Level 1 Lesson 7

零 líng zero, 0
现在 xiànzài now
几点 jǐdiǎn what time
周末 zhōumò weekend
做 zuò to do
什么 shénme what
看 kàn to watch, to look

Example Sentence and Dialogue to Practice

现在几点了？ xiàn zài jǐ diǎn le what time is it now?
十一点十八分 shí yī diǎn shí bā fēn 11:18
你周末做什么? nǐ zhōu mò zuò shén me What do you do on weekends?
周末我游泳 zhōu mò wǒ yóu yǒng I swim on weekends.
九点 起床 jiǔ diǎn qǐ chuáng get up at 9

Dialogue

现在几点了？ xiàn zài jǐ diǎn le What time is it now?
四点半。 sì diǎn bàn 4:30
什么？ 六点半吗？ shén me? liù diǎn bàn ma? What? 6:30?
不是六点半， 是四点半 bú shì liù diǎn bàn, shì sì diǎn bàn No, it’s not 6:30, it’s 4:30
你几点去吃饭？ nǐ jǐ diǎn qù chī fàn What time you going to eat?
我六点去吃饭。你呢？ wǒ liù diǎn qù chī fàn. Nǐ ne? I’m going to eat at 6:00. How about you?
我不去吃饭， 我去游泳。 wǒ bú qù chī fàn, wǒ qù yóu yǒng I’m not going to eat, I’m going to swim.
