Key Chinese Vocabulary Level 1 Lesson 10

旅游 lǚyóu to travel
英国 yīngguó England
两点 liǎng diǎn 2 o’clock
今年 jīn nián this year
去年 qù nián last year
明年 míng nián next year
这 zhè this
晚上 wǎnshàng evening
睡觉 shuìjiào sleep
对不起 duìbùqǐ I’m sorry
祝贺 zhùhè to congratulate

Example Sentence and Dialogue to Practice

今年是2014年， 去年是2013年, 明年是2015年. jīnnián shì èrlíngyīsìnián, qùnián shì èrlíngyīsān nián, míngnián shì èrlíngyīwǔnián This year is 2014, last year was 2013, next year is 2015.
吃三明治好吗？ chī sānmíngzhì hǎoma? Is eating sandwich OK for you?
祝贺你 zhùhènǐ congratulations to you

Dialogue 1

我喜欢旅游，你呢？ wǒ xǐhuān lǚyóu, nǐne I like traveling, how about you?
我也是。去年我去了中国。今年我去了英国，明年我去日本。 wǒyěshì, qùnián wǒ qùle zhōngguó. jīnnián wǒ qùle yīngguó, míngnián wǒ qù rìběn I also (like traveling). Last year I went to China. This year I went to England, next year I’m going to Japan.
明年几月你去日本？ míngnián jǐyuè nǐqù rìběn What month will you go to Japan next year?
明年九月十五号是我朋友的生日。我九月去日本。今年你去旅游吗？ míngnián jiǔyuè shíwǔhào shì wǒ péngyǒude shēngrì. wǒ jiǔyuè qù rìběn.
jīnnián nǐ qù lǚyóuma?		 Next year September 15th is my friend’s birthday. I will go to Japan in
September. Are you traveling this year?
这个周末我去巴西看世界杯 zhège zhōumò wǒ qù bāxī kàn shìjièbēi This weekend I will go to Brazil to watch the World Cup
什么？去看世界杯？ shénme? qù kàn shìjièbēi What? Go see the World Cup?
对，我喜欢看世界杯。你呢？ duì, wǒ xǐhuān kàn shìjièbēi. nǐne? Yes, I like to watch the World Cup. How about you?
我也喜欢看世界杯，我不去巴西，我看电视…. wǒyě xǐhuān kàn shìjièbēi, wǒ búqù bāxī, wǒ kàn diànshì I also like to watch the World Cup, I’m not going to Brazil, I’m watching (it) on TV.

Dialogue 2

明天我们去游泳吧 míngtián wǒmen qù yóuyǒngba Tomorrow let’s go swimming
好。几点？ hǎo. Jǐdiǎn Ok. What time?
下午四点半好吗？ xiàwǔ sìdiǎnbàn hǎoma? 4:30pm good?
四点半我工作，上午九点一刻好吗？ sìdiǎnbàn wǒ gōngzuò, shàngwǔ jiǔdiǎn yīkè hǎoma 4:30 I work, Is 9:15am ok?
上午我学习，晚上呢？ shàngwǔ wǒ xuéxí, wǎnshàngne I study in the morning, how about in the evening?
明天晚上六点我见朋友，八点半好吗？ míngtiān wǎnshàng liùdiǎn wǒ jiàn péngyǒu, bādiǎnbàn hǎoma Tomorrow evening at 6 I will meet a friend, (is) 8:30 ok?
不好，我八点睡觉 bùhǎo, wǒ bādiǎn shuìjiào Not good, I sleep at 8.
什么？八点睡觉!? shénme? bādiǎn shuìjiào What? Sleep at 8!?
对不起，明天我不去游泳…. duìbùqǐ, míngtiān wǒ búqù yóuyǒng I’m sorry, tomorrow I’m not going swimming….
