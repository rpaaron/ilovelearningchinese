Level 1 Lesson 13 – How is the weather? / 天气怎么样？


In this free Chinese lesson you will learn to ask about weather and tell about the weather at your location or a place that you have been to.  So let’s begin lesson 13 of this beginner Chinese series. Key Vocabulary Level 1 Lesson 13

天气 tiānqì Weather
洛杉矶 luòshānjī Los Angeles
冷 lěng Cold
热 rè hot
下雨 xiàyǔ rain
风 fēng wind
大风 dàfēng very windy
空调 kōngtiáo air condition
度 dù degree
多少 duōshǎo how much, how many
多少度? duōshǎodù What’s the temperature?
一百 yībǎi 100
春天 chūntiān spring
夏天 xiàtiān summer
秋天 qiūtiān fall
冬天 dōngtiān winter

Example Sentence and Dialogue to Practice

明天天气怎么样？ míngtiān tiānqì zěnméyàng how is the weather tomorrow?
北京冬天很冷 běijīng dōngtiān hěnlěng Beijing’s winter is very cold
洛杉矶不太热 luòshānjī bútàirè LA weather is not too hot.
昨天下雨了吗？ zuótiān xiàyǔlema Did it rain yesterday?
我不喜欢大风大雨 wǒ bù xǐhuān dàfēng dàyǔ I don’t like very windy or heavy rain

Dialogue

今年你旅游了吗？ jiānnián nǐ lǚyóulema Did you travel this year?
我去了洛杉矶，你呢？ wǒqùle luòshānjī, nǐne I went to Los Angeles, how about you?
我七月去了上海见朋友 wǒ qīyuě qùle shànghǎi jiàn péngyou In July I went to see a friend in Shanghai.
上海天气怎么样？ shànghǎi tiānqì zěnméyàng How was the weather in Shanghai?
上海夏天太热了。 shànghǎi xiàtiān tàirèle Shanghai’s summer was very hot.
多少度？ duōshǎodù What’s the temperature?
一百度 yībǎidù 100 degrees.
晚上也热吗？ wǎnshàng yěrèlema Was it also hot in the evening?
晚上也很热。洛杉矶天气怎么样？ wǎnshàng yě hěnrè. luòshānjī tiānqì zěnméyàng It’s also hot in the evening. How is the weather in LA?
洛杉矶六月天气很好，不冷也不热。早上和晚上七十度， 有风。 luòshānjī liùyuè tiānqì hěnhǎo, bùlěng yě búrè. zǎoshàng hé
wǎnshàng qīshídù, yǒufēng		 LA in June is very good, not too cold and not too hot. In the morning
and evening its 70 degrees and windy.
天气太好了！我喜欢洛杉矶的天气，明年我也去洛杉矶。 tiānqì tàihǎole! wǒ xǐhuān luòshānjīde tiānqì, míngnián wǒ yě qù luòshānjī The weather’s great! I like LA’s weather, next year I also will go to LA.
