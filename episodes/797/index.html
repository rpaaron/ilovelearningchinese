Level 1 Lesson 16 – I Would like Coffee / 我要咖啡


In this Chinese lesson you will learn the three uses of 要 (yào) to express what you’d like to have, what you are going to do or what needs to be done.  So let’s begin lesson 16 of this beginner Chinese series. Be sure to listen to our Chinese podcast and if you haven’t yet, subscribe to our Chinese itunes podcast so you can get the next lesson immediately when it’s published. If you liked this lesson please take a moment to like us on facebook

Example Sentence and Dialogue to Practice

今天我们要学什么？ jīntiān wǒmen yào xué shénme? What are we going to study today?
明天我要去看电影 míngtiān wǒ yào qù kàn diànyǐng Tomorrow I’m going to watch a movie
九月我要搬家 jiúyuè wǒ yào bānjiā I’m going to move in September
后年我要学法语 hòunián wǒ yào xué fǎyǔ I’m going to learn French two years later.
我要冰激淋 wǒ yào bīngjīlín I’d like to have ice cream
我要咖啡和牛奶 wǒ yào kāfēi hé niúnǎi I’d like to have coffee and milk
明天很忙，我要工作 míngtiān hěnmáng, wǒ yào gōngzuò tomorrow I’ll be busy. I have to work
明天下雨，你要有雨伞 míngtiān xiàyǔ, nǐ yào yǒu yǔsǎn it’s going to rain tomorrow. You need to have an umbrella

Dialogue 1

先生，您要什么？ xiānsheng, nín yào shénme Mr. what would you like?`
我要咖啡 wǒ yào kāfēi I’d like to have coffee
还要什么？ hái yào shénme What else would you like to have?
有苹果汁吗？ yǒu píngguǒzhīma Do you have apple juice?
有 yǒu We have it.
好，我要冷的苹果汁 hǎo, wǒ yào lěngde píngguǒzhī good, I’d like cold apple juice
咖啡呢，要冷的还是热的？ kāfēine, yào lěngde háishì rède What about the coffee, would you like it cold or hot?
咖啡要热的。我要带走 kāfēi yào rède. wǒ yào dàizǒu The coffee should be hot. I’m going take it to go

Dialogue 2

周末你要做什么? zhōumò nǐ yào zuò shénme What are you going to do on the weekend?
周末我要学中文。你有空吗？我们看中文电影吧 zhōumò wǒ yào xué zhōngwén. nǐ yǒu kòngma? wǒmen kàn zhōngwén
diànyǐngba		 I’m going to study Chinese in the weekend. Are you available?
Let’s watch a Chinese movie
对不起，这周很忙。周末我要加班。 duìbùqǐ, zhè zhōu hěnmáng. zhōumò wǒ yào jiābān I’m sorry, this week I’m busy. I need to work over time on the weekend
你要好好休息啊。 nǐ yào hǎohǎo xiūxi ā You better rest well.
是啊，好好休息，也要多运动。 shì ā, hǎohǎo xiūxi, yě yào duōyùndòng Yes, rest well and also have to work out more.
