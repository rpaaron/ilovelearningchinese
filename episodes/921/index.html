Level 1 Lesson 20 – 失而复得 / Lost Something but Found it (Review)


In this free Chinese lesson you will review what you learned from lessons 16-19. If you were able to keep up with all the lessons, congratulations, you have come a long way.  You should be able to express a lot with what you have learned so far. And lesson 20 will reinforce what you learned.  So let’s begin lesson/review 20 of this beginner Chinese series. Be sure to listen to our Chinese podcast and if you haven’t yet, subscribe to our Chinese itunes podcast so you can get the next lesson immediately when it’s published. If you liked this lesson please take a moment to like us on facebook

Example Sentence and Dialogue to Practice

我要一杯茶 wǒ yào yībēi chá I’d like to have a cup of tea
九月我要去纽约上大学 jiǔ yuè wǒ yào qù niǔyuē shàng dàxué I’m going to NY for college in Sept
你要多运动 nǐ yào duō yùndòng You should work out more.
我可以用你的手机吗？ wǒ kěyǐ yòng nǐde shǒujī ma Can I use your cell phone?
你能不能帮我买东西？ nǐ néng bù néng bāng wǒ mǎi dōngxī Can you help me buy something or not?
我能说葡萄牙语 wǒ néng shuō pútáoyá yǔ I’m able to speak Portuguese
我学中文，你也学中文，我们都爱学中文 wǒ xué zhōngwén, nǐ yě xué zhōngwén, wǒmen dōu ài xué zhōngwén I study Chinese, you also study Chinese, we all love to learn Chinese.
妈妈在家，爸爸也在家，我们都在家 māma zài jiā, bàba yě zài jiā, wǒmen dōu zài jiā Mom is at home, dad is also at home, we are all at home.
机票多少钱？ jīpiào duōshǎo qián how much is the flight ticket
机票打折吗？ jīpiào dǎ zhé ma Is there discount for flight ticket?
圣诞节机票贵 shèngdànjié jīpiào guì Flight tickets are expensive during Christmas

Dialogue 1

你的生日是几月几号？ nǐde shēngrì shì jǐyuè jǐhào What day and month is your birthday?
我的生日是十月九号 wǒde shēngrì shì shíyuè jiǔ hào My birthday is on October 9th
是周六，一起去吃饭吧 shì zhōu liù, yīqǐ qù chī fàn ba It’s on Saturday, let’s go eat a meal together.
去哪里？ qù nǎlǐ Where to go?
去steakhouse，我买单 qù steakhouse wǒ mǎi dān (Let’s) go to steakhouse, It’s my treat.
很贵吧 hěn guì ba It’s expensive.
我有打折券，很便宜。 wǒ yǒu dǎzhéquàn, hěn piányì I have a discount coupon, (its) very cheap
好啊，我们可以下午见面吗？上午我要工作。 hǎo ā, wǒmen kěyǐ xiàwǔ jiànmiàn ma? shàngwǔ wǒ yào gōngzuò Ok, can we meet in the afternoon? In the morning I have work.
上午我也很忙，下午五点见。 shàngwǔ wǒ yě hěn máng, xiàwǔ wǔdiǎn jiàn In the morning I’m also very busy, 5pm (let’s) meet.
好，五点见 hǎo, wǔdiǎn jiàn Ok, see (you) 5 o’clock

Dialogue 2

老板，苹果多少钱？ lǎobǎn píngguǒ duōshǎo qián Boss, an apple costs how much?
四块，要不要？ sìkuài, yàobúyào 4 dollars, you want it or not?
太贵了，便宜一点吧？ tàiguìle, piányì yīdiǎn ba It’s too expensive, (can you go) a little cheaper?
我的苹果很好，不打折。 wǒde píngguǒ hěn hǎo, bù dǎzhé My apple is very good, no discount
打折吧，我买多一点 dǎzhé ba, wǒ mǎi duō yīdiǎn (give a) discount, I’ll buy a little more.
多少钱你要？ duōshǎo qián nǐ yào How much do you want (the apple for)?
三块怎么样？你的苹果我都买。 sānkuài zěnme yàng? nǐde píngguǒ wǒ dōu mǎi 3 dollars how about it? I’ll buy all your apples
好吧。香蕉也买一点吧，打折。 hǎo ba. xiāngjiāo yě mǎi yīdiǎn ba, dǎzhé Alright. Also buy some bananas discounted.
香蕉多少钱？ xiāngjiāo duō shǎo qián Bananas are how much?
香蕉一块怎么样？ xiāngjiāo yīkuài zěnme yàng Bananas are 1 dollar how about it?
好，你的香蕉我都要 hǎo, nǐde xiāngjiāo wǒ dōuyào Ok, I want all your bananas
谢谢，怎么买这么多水果？ xièxiè, zěnme mǎi zhème duō shuǐguǒ Thanks, how come (you) buy this much fruit?
后天妹妹生日，有很多朋友，我要做苹果派和香蕉蛋糕 hòutiān mèimei shēngri, yǒu hěn duō péngyou, wǒ yào zuò píngguǒ
pài hé xiāngjiāo dàngāo		 The day after tomorrow is my younger sister’s birthday, there’s a lot
of friends, I’m going to make apple pie and banana cake.
你是好姐姐！ nǐshì hǎo jiějie You’re a good older sister!
